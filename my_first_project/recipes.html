Grilled Fish

Perfectly seasoned fish grilled to smoky perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized fish (tilapia or mackerel)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon pepper blend
  • 1 teaspoon garlic and ginger paste
  • Salt and seasoning to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Recipe

  1. Clean and score the fish on both sides.
  2. Mix spices, oil, and pepper to make a marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the fish and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Grill in the oven or on charcoal for 20‚Äì25 minutes, turning once.
  5. Serve hot with sauce or rice.

jollof

served with plantain and chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes, salt, and curry powder

Recipe

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions together.
  2. Fry the mixture in oil until the water reduces.
  3. Add seasoning and spices to taste.
  4. Add washed rice and stir well.
  5. Pour in water, cover, and cook on low heat until done.
